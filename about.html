About

Independent reviews, transparent methodology.

SpykeSIK

SpykeVision is an independent, reader-supported home-theater publication. I review projectors the way I'd want them reviewed: with real measurements, full transparency about test conditions, and no marketing spin. If a number looks too good, I tell you why, and how I got it.

How I measure

Every review is built on a consistent bench process so results are comparable across products:

  • Brightness: measured in calibrated picture modes (not the brightest factory preset), across the full zoom and aperture range, after thermal stabilization.
  • Native contrast: full-field on/off, per zoom and aperture position.
  • ANSI contrast: 4×4 checkerboard, measured at both centered and shifted lens positions.
  • ADL contrast: across content levels from on/off through 50% APL.
  • Optics: sharpness patterns, distortion via laser level, uniformity maps, and lens-shift behavior.
Transparency first: if you want to reproduce a measurement, the test pattern, meter and conditions are documented alongside each chart. Spotted something off? Get in touch; corrections are published openly.

Get in touch

Reviews also appear on the AVS Forum, where discussion is always welcome. For review requests, corrections or manufacturer inquiries, reach out via the forum or email.
