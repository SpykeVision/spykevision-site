SpykeVision is an independent, reader-supported home-theater publication. I review projectors the way I'd want them reviewed: with real measurements, full transparency about test conditions, and no marketing spin. If a number looks too good or too bad, I tell you why, and how I measured it.

## How I measure

Every review is built on a consistent bench process so results are comparable across products:

- **Brightness:** measured in manufacturer-calibrated picture modes, across the full zoom and aperture range, after thermal stabilization.
- **Native contrast:** Full on/off, per zoom and aperture position.
- **ANSI contrast:** 8×8 pattern, measured at both centered and shifted lens positions.
- **ADL contrast:** across scene brightness levels from On/Off through 50% (ANSI).
- **Optics:** sharpness patterns, distortion via laser level, uniformity maps, and lens-shift behavior.

<div class="note">The test pattern, meter, and conditions are documented alongside each chart — so anyone can reproduce a measurement. Spotted something off? Write to me — corrections are published openly.</div>
